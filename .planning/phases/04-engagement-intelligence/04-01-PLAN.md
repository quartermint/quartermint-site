<objective>
Make the chat contextually aware of what the visitor is looking at by tracking scroll position via IntersectionObserver, passing section context to the chat API, injecting it into the system prompt, and reflecting it in dynamic starter chips.

Purpose: This is the foundation of engagement intelligence -- the chat stops being a static widget and starts responding to visitor behavior. Scroll context feeds both the AI responses and the chip suggestions.
Output: ScrollContextProvider wrapping the page, scroll-aware chat API, dynamic starter chips, section-to-context mappings.
</objective>
