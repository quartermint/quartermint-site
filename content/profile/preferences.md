### NEVER NAME CLIENT ORGANIZATIONS

- Exception: public-record campaign committees (Obama 2008, Biden-Harris 2024) can be named

- Specific financial details, dollar amounts, or compensation
- Relationship dynamics beyond what's professionally relevant
