## Summary

Treasurer and operations lead for Battle Born Collective, a C4 organization focused on progressive political work. Ryan retained BBC after the New Deal Strategies engagement failed, describing it as a chance to "just sort of lick my wounds. I can help one group and it isn't really growing a company anymore." Was effectively BBC's in-house everything utility guy, but the outfit was small enough that he could manage it by himself. Managed payroll operations through Poliops LLC, handled compliance accounting, and built financial systems. The team built here went on to become senior staff on John Fetterman's successful U.S. Senate campaign.
