## Summary

Treasurer and operations lead for a C4 organization focused on progressive political work. Ryan retained this role after the consulting firm engagement failed, describing it as a chance to "just sort of lick my wounds. I can help one group and it isn't really growing a company anymore." Was effectively the organization's in-house everything utility guy, but the outfit was small enough that he could manage it by himself. Managed payroll operations through a dedicated LLC, handled compliance accounting, and built financial systems. The team built here went on to become senior staff on a successful U.S. Senate campaign.
